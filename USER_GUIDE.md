## Features Overview

Presenton provides a complete AI presentation generation platform with:

- **AI-Powered Content Generation** — Automatic slide creation with intelligent content
- **Multiple AI Models** — Choose from free or premium models based on your needs
- **Image Generation** — Automatic images for each slide (optional)
- **Metrics Dashboard** — Track your presentation creation activity
- **Analytics Reports** — Understand your usage patterns
- **Multiple Export Formats** — Download as PPTX or PDF
- **Presentation History** — Access all your created presentations
- **Customizable Templates** — Choose from multiple design themes
- **Multi-Language Support** — Create presentations in 15+ languages

## Creating Presentations

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Access Presenton

Open your browser and navigate to:
```
http://localhost:5000
```

#### 2. Enter Your Topic

In the main text box, describe what you want your presentation about:

**Examples:**
- "Introduction to Machine Learning"
- "Q4 Sales Report 2025"
- "Climate Change: Causes and Solutions"
- "How to Start a Podcast"

#### 3. Configure Settings

**Number of Slides**
- Range: 5-20 slides
- Default: 8 slides
- Tip: Fewer slides = faster generation

**Language**
- Select from 15+ supported languages
- Default: English
- Presentations will be generated in your selected language

**Template**
- Choose your presentation design
- Options: General, Business, Creative, Minimal, and more
- You can change templates after generation

**Tone** (Optional)
- Default: Professional
- Options: Casual, Professional, Funny, Educational, Sales Pitch

**Verbosity** (Optional)
- Concise: Short, punchy content
- Standard: Balanced content (default)
- Text-Heavy: Detailed, comprehensive content

#### 4. Generate

Click the **Generate** button and wait for completion.

**Generation Time**:
- 5 slides: 1-2 minutes
- 8 slides: 2-3 minutes
- 15 slides: 3-5 minutes

Times vary based on model selection and system load.

#### 5. Review

Once generated, you can:
- **View** the presentation in Presenton
- **Edit** individual slides
- **Download** as PPTX or PDF
- **Present** directly in the app

## Understanding Model Selection

### What is a Model?

A model is the AI engine that generates your presentation content. Different models have different:
- **Speed** — How fast they generate content
- **Quality** — How good the content is
- **Cost** — How much they charge per use

### Free vs Premium Models

**Free Models**
- Cost: $0
- Speed: 2-5 minutes per presentation
- Quality: Good for most use cases
- Examples: Meta Llama, Google Gemma, Mistral

**Premium Models**
- Cost: $0.01-$0.50 per presentation
- Speed: 2-4 minutes per presentation
- Quality: Excellent, more detailed content
- Examples: Claude, GPT-4, Gemini Pro

### How to Choose

**Use Free Models If:**
- You're testing or learning
- You have a limited budget
- Speed isn't critical
- Content quality is acceptable

**Use Premium Models If:**
- You need high-quality content
- You're creating for clients
- You need specific expertise
- You want faster generation

### Default Configuration

Presenton uses **free models by default** to keep costs at $0. You can upgrade to premium models by:

1. Going to **Settings**
2. Selecting a premium model
3. Providing your API key (if required)

## Exporting Presentations

### Download as PowerPoint (PPTX)

**Steps:**
1. Open your presentation
2. Click **Download PPTX**
3. Save to your computer
4. Open in PowerPoint, Google Slides, or LibreOffice

**Benefits:**
- Fully editable in PowerPoint
- Compatible with all presentation software
- Preserves all formatting and images
- Can be further customized

### Download as PDF

**Steps:**
1. Open your presentation
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. Save to your computer
4. Open in any PDF viewer

**Benefits:**
- Fixed format (won't change)
- Easy to share
- Can be printed directly
- Smaller file size

### Choosing Format

| Format | Best For | Editable |
|--------|----------|----------|
| PPTX | Editing, customizing | ✅ Yes |
| PDF | Sharing, printing | ❌ No |

## Best Practices for Content

### Writing Effective Topics

**Good Topics:**
- "Introduction to Machine Learning"
- "Q4 2025 Sales Report"
- "How to Start a Podcast"
- "Climate Change: Causes and Solutions"

**Avoid:**
- Too vague: "Stuff about business"
- Too specific: "Why my company's Q4 sales were 15.3% higher than Q3"
- Too long: Stick to 5-10 words

### Tips for Better Results

1. **Be Specific** — More detail = better content
2. **Use Keywords** — Include important terms
3. **Set Expectations** — Mention audience level (beginner, advanced)
4. **Provide Context** — "For a tech conference" or "For high school students"

### Examples

**Basic:**
```
"Introduction to Python"
```

**Better:**
```
"Introduction to Python for Beginners"
```

**Best:**
```
"Introduction to Python for Beginners - Web Development Focus"
```

## Performance Tips

### Faster Generation

1. **Use Fewer Slides** — 5-8 slides generate faster than 15-20
2. **Use Free Models** — Faster than premium models
3. **Simpler Topics** — Straightforward topics generate faster
4. **Disable Images** — Presentations without images generate faster

### Better Quality

1. **Use Premium Models** — Higher quality content
2. **More Slides** — More content = more detail
3. **Specific Topics** — Detailed topics = better content
4. **Enable Images** — Visual presentations look better

### Balancing Speed and Quality

| Need | Recommendation |
|------|-----------------|
| Fast + Good | 8 slides, free model, images enabled |
| Fast + Best | 8 slides, premium model, images enabled |
| Detailed + Good | 15 slides, free model, images enabled |
| Detailed + Best | 15 slides, premium model, images enabled |

## Editing Presentations

### Edit Individual Slides

1. Open a presentation
2. Click **Edit** on any slide
3. Modify text, images, or layout
4. Changes save automatically

### Edit Slide Content

- **Title**: Click to edit

- Use **Ctrl+Z** (Windows) or **Cmd+Z** (Mac)
- Or click **Undo** button if available

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Presentation takes too long**
- This is normal for 15+ slides
- Free models are slower than premium
- Try reducing slide count

**API errors**
- Check your internet connection
- Verify API keys are correct
- See [Troubleshooting Guide](TROUBLESHOOTING_GUIDE.md)

**Images not generating**
- Image generation may be disabled
- Check configuration settings
- See [Configuration Guide](CONFIGURATION_GUIDE.md)

**Poor quality content**
- Try a premium model
- Be more specific in your topic
- Increase slide count for more detail

For more help, see [Troubleshooting Guide](TROUBLESHOOTING_GUIDE.md)
